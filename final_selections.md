# Final Selections

**Budget Manager’s Top Idea:**
- Print-on-Demand Production
- *Reasoning*: Controls costs and risk, minimizes waste, adapts to fluctuating demand. Most flexible model for a niche product line.

**Market Research Expert’s Top Idea:**
- Eco-Friendly Positioning
- *Reasoning*: Matches growing demand for sustainability and custom products in home decor. Essential for both PR and authentic branding.

**Product Design Expert’s Top Idea:**
- Modular, Stackable Designs
- *Reasoning*: Versatile, scalable for diverse spaces, and encourages repeat purchases. Visual appeal and engagement are high; complexity is a manageable tradeoff.

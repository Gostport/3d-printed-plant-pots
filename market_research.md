# Market Research Expert Ideas

1. **Eco-Friendly Positioning**
   - Market as sustainable, customizable plant pots using biodegradable or recycled filament options.
   - *Rationale*: Many consumers are moving towards sustainability and personalized products, especially for home decor.

2. **Niche Targeting for Urban Gardening**
   - Target apartment dwellers and urban hobbyists who prefer compact, stylish pots for small spaces or balconies.
   - *Rationale*: Urban gardening is trending, and space-saving designs appeal to this growing segment.

3. **Social Media and Maker Community Engagement**
   - Leverage platforms like Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok to showcase designs, printing process, and customer creations.
   - *Rationale*: Maker communities and visually-driven social platforms drive organic growth and inspire UGC.
